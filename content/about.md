Welcome to my website! I'm Moray, currently a PhD student at the [James Hutton Institute](https://www.hutton.ac.uk/).
I make blog posts extremely infrequently, find 'em here.

My current research is spread over a couple of topics - plant disease resistance; plant genomics, transcriptomics, epigenomics, mobilomics; soil metabarcoding and metagenomics; also a lot of nanopore sequencing.
I create a lot of data analysis pipelines, mostly with [snakemake](https://snakemake.readthedocs.io/en/stable/), and am passionate about the benefits they bring with usability, understandability, and data integrity.

"The Cowboy Zone" refers to the zone of research/programming/life where nothing is really working and you have no idea what is going on 🤠.
